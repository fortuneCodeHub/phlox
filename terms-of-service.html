Payments

Brand payments: All campaign budgets must be deposited in full before sourcing begins. The 15% platform service fee is deducted from the campaign budget on activation. Phlox uses Flutterwave to process brand deposits.

Creator payouts: Creators are paid via Flutterwave bank transfer to their registered Nigerian bank account after their content is approved and goes live. Payouts are typically processed within 1-3 business days of approval.

Refunds: If a campaign is cancelled before sourcing begins, the full budget is refunded to the brand wallet. If sourcing has already begun, the platform service fee is non-refundable. Unused creator fees are returned to the brand wallet.

Subscription fees: Monthly subscription charges are non-refundable. Downgrading a plan takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
